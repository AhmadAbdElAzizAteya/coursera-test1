Kalu the thirsty crow

Thirsty

He flew about here and there looking for water. It was a hot summer day. Kalu the crow was very thirsty. He saw a pot with water. He went close to the pot.

Water

Kalu saw the it was very little water in the pot. Kalu could not reach the water. “ what do I do. I’m very thirsty.” Kalu looked around. He saw some pebbles.

Happy

He picked up the pebbles one by one and dropped them into the pot. As Kalu dropped the pebbles into the water, the water came up. Kalu drank the water. He flew away happily.
